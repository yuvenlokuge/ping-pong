Ai ping pong game






Devloped by YUVEN

Instruction

  1. first keep your laptop screen straight
  2. move yourself aproximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  3. move your right wrist in a ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on the right wrist
  4. now press the play button and again move yourself 3-4 feet away form the laptop
  5. the red paddle is your paddle that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
